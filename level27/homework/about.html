About Facebook

Introduction

Facebook is a social networking service launched in February 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates. It allows users to create profiles, connect with friends, share photos, videos, and updates.

History

Originally limited to Harvard students, Facebook rapidly expanded to other universities and then to the general public. Today, it is one of the largest social media platforms worldwide.

Features

Facebook offers a wide range of features including News Feed, Messenger, Marketplace, Groups, Events, and many others, making it a central hub for social interaction.
